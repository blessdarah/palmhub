About palmhub

Palmhub is an enterprise which deals in the supply of high quality organic palm oil and its by-products such as palm kennel, palm kennel shell, brooms and palm nut chaffs. Our products com straight form Widikum which has a renown reputation as the best producer of palm oil in the North West region of Cameroon. We supply in large quantities per wholesale and small quantities for retailing and personal home consumption. Our greatest desire is to give our customers satisfaction that is why we are connected with over 300 farmers to deliver unmixed and 100% organic quality palm oil from Widikum.
